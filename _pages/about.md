## Welcome to my blog!

I am an offensive security professional currently working full-time as a Pentester. I’ve always been passionate about challenging myself through CTFs and sharing my findings so others can learn and grow.

Now that I am working in the industry, I want this blog to reflect that evolution. I am shifting the focus to bring more practical value to the community. Alongside my usual write-ups, I will be sharing real-world pentesting tricks, methodology shortcuts, and honest reviews of industry certifications to help you navigate your own career path.
